ShreeJñāneshwarī Adhyay 5 Transliterated

|| ShreeJñāneshwarī ||

Adhyāya 5 vā

Arjuna uvācha - Saṁ(n)nyāsaṁ(ṅ) karmaṇāṁ(ṅ) Kriṣhṇa punaryogaṁ(ñ) cha shaṁsasi | yachchhreya etayorekaṁ(n) tanme brūhi sunishchitam || Bha.Gī.5.1 ||

[Arjuna uvācha - saṁ(n)nyāsaṁ(ṅ) karmaṇāṁ(ṅ) Kriṣhṇa] Maga Pārth ShreeKriṣhṇāte mhaṇe | hāṁho he kaise tumache bolaṇe | eka hoya tarī aṅtaḥkaraṇe | vichārū ye || Jñāne.5.1 || Māgā sakaḷa karmāṅchā saṅnyāsa | tumhīṅcha niropilā hotā bahuvasa | [punaryogaṁ(ñ) cha shaṁsasi] tarī karmayogī kevī atirasa | pokhītasā puḍhatī || Jñāne.5.2 || Aise vyartha he bolatā | āmhā neṇatayāṅchyā chittā | āpuliye chāḍe Shreeanaṅtā | umaja nohe || Jñāne.5.3 || Aike ekasārāte bodhije | tarī ekaniṣhṭhachi bolije | he āṇikī kāya sāṅgije | tumhāṁprati || Jñāne.5.4 || Tarī yāchilāgī tumate | myā rāuḷāsī vinavile hote | je hā paramārtha dhwanite | na bolāvā || Jñāne.5.5 || Parī māgīla aso devā | [yachchhreya etayorekamiti] ātā prastutī ukala dekhāvā | sāṅge dohīṁmāji baravā | mārga kavaṇa || Jñāne.5.6 || Ĵo pariṇāmīṅchā nirvāḷā | achuṁbita ye phaḷā | āṇi anuṣhṭhitā prāṅjaḷā | sāviyāchi || Jñāne.5.7 || Jaise nidreche sukha na moḍe | āṇi mārga tarī bahusāla sāṅḍe | taise sukhāsanā sāṅgaḍe | sohape hoya || Jñāne.5.8 || [Bhaktirahasyaṁ] Yeṇe Arjunācheni bole | devo manī rijhale | maga hoīla aike mhaṇitale | saṅtoṣhoniyā || Jñāne.5.9 || Dekhā Kāmadhenuaisī māye | sadaivā jayā hoye | to chaṅdrahī parī lāhe | kheḷāvayā || Jñāne.5.10 || Pāhe pā Shreeshaṁbhūchī prasannatā | tayā upamanyūchiyā ārtā | kāya kṣhīrābdhi dūdhabhātā | deijechinā || Jñāne.5.11 || Taisā audāryāĉhā kuruṭhā | ShreeKriṣhṇa āpu jāhāliyā subhaṭā | kā sarva sukhāchā vasauṭā | tochi nohāvā || Jñāne.5.12 || Etha chamatkāra kāyasā | gosāvī Shreelakṣhmīkāṅtā aisā | ātā āpuliyā savesā | māgāvā kī || Jñāne.5.13 || Mhaṇoni Arjune mhaṇitale | te hāṁsoni yere didhale | techi sāṅgena bolile | kāya Kriṣhṇe || Jñāne.5.14 ||

Shrībhagavānuvācha - Saṁ(n)nyāsaḥ(kh) karmayogashcha niḥ(hi)shreyasakarāvubhau | tayostu karmasaṁ(n)nyāsātkarmayogo vishiṣhyate || Bha.Gī.5.2 ||

[Shrībhagavānuvācha-saṁ(n)nyāsa iti] To mhaṇe gā kuṅtīsutā | he saṅnyāsa yoga vichāritā | mokṣhakara tattwatā | donīhī hotī || Jñāne.5.15 || [Tayoriti] Tarī jāṇāṅneṇā sakaḷā | hā karmayoga kīra prāṅjaḷā | jaisī nāva striyā bāḷā | toyataraṇī || Jñāne.5.16 || Taise sārāsāra pāhije | tarī sohapā hāchi dekhije | yeṇe saṅnyāsaphaḷa lāhije | anāyāse || Jñāne.5.17 || [Chūrṇikā] Ātā yāchilāgī sāṅgena | tuĵa saṅnyāsiyāche chihna | maga sahaĵe he abhinna | ĵāṇasī tū || Jñāne.5.18 ||

Jñeyaḥ(s) sa nityasaṁ(n)nyāsī yo na dweṣhṭi na kāṅgkṣhati | nirdwandwo hi mahābāho sukhaṁ bandhātpramuchyate || Bha.Gī.5.3 ||

[Na kāṅgkṣhati] Tarī geliyāchī se na karī | na pavatā ĉhāḍa na dharī | ĵo sunishchaḷa aṅtarī | Meru jaisā || Jñāne.5.19 || [Nirdwandwo hi mahābāho] Āṇi mī māĵhe aisī āṭhavaṇa | visarale jayāche aṅtaḥkaraṇa | [jñeyaḥ(s) sa nityasaṁ(n)nyāsī] Pārthā to saṅnyāsī ĵāṇa | niraṅtara || Jñāne.5.20 || Ĵo mane aisā ĵāhalā | saṅgī tochi sāṅḍilā | [sukhaṁ] mhaṇoni sukhe sukha pāvalā | akhaṅḍita || Jñāne.5.21 || Ātā gruhādika āghave | te kāhī nalage tyajāve | je ghete ĵāhāle swabhāve | niḥsaṅga mhaṇaūni || Jñāne.5.22 || [Bandhātpramuchyate] Dekhe agni vijhoni jāye | maga je rākhoṅḍī kevaḷa hoye | tai te kāpuse givasū ye | jiyāparī || Jñāne.5.23 || Taisā asateni upādhī | nākaḷije to karmabaṅdhī | jayāchiye buddhī | saṅkalpa nāhī || Jñāne.5.24 || [Chūrṇikā] Mhaṇoni kalpanā jai sāṅḍe | taiṅchi gā saṅnyāsa ghaḍe | iye kāraṇe donī sāṅgaḍe | saṅnyāsa yoga || Jñāne.5.25 ||

Sāṅgkhyayogau pruthagbālāḥ(ph) pravadanti na paṇḍitāḥ(hā) | ekamapyāsthitaḥ(s) samyagubhayorvindate phalam || Bha.Gī.5.4 ||

[Sāṅgkhyayogau pruthagbālāḥ(ph) pravadanti] Yerhavī tarī Pārthā | je mūrkha hotī sarvathā | te sāṅkhyayogasaṁsthā | ĵāṇatī kevī || Jñāne.5.26 || Sahaĵe te ajñāna | mhaṇoni mhaṇatī te bhinna | [na paṇḍitāḥ(hā)] yerhavī dīpāprati kāi ānāna | prakāsha āhātī || Jñāne.5.27 || [Ekamapyāsthita iti] Pai samyaka eke anubhave | jihī dekhile tattwa āghave | dohīṅteṁhī aikyabhāve | mānitī gā || Jñāne.5.28 ||

Yatsāṅgkhyaiḥ(ph) prāpyate sthānaṁ(n) tadyogairapi gamyate | ekaṁ sāṅgkhyaṁ(ñ) cha yogaṁ(ñ) cha yaḥ(ph) pashyati sa pashyati || Bha.Gī.5.5 ||

[Yatsāṅgkhyairiti] Āṇi sāṅkhyī je pāvije | techi yogī gamije | mhaṇoni aikya dohīṅte sahaĵe | iyāparī || Jñāne.5.29 || [Ekamiti] Dekhe ākāshā āṇi avakāshā | bheda nāhī jaisā | taise aikya yogasaṅnyāsā | voḷakhe ĵo || Jñāne.5.30 || Tayāsīchi jagī pāhale | āpaṇape teṇechi dekhile | jayā sāṅkhya yoga ĵāṇavale | bhedeṁviṇa || Jñāne.5.31 ||

Saṁ(n)nyāsastu mahābāho duḥ(hu)khamāptumayogataḥ | yogayukto munirbrahma nachireṇādhigachchhati || Bha.Gī.5.6 ||

[Yogayukta iti] Ĵo yuktipaṅthe Pārthā | chaḍhe mokṣhaparvatā | to mahāsukhāĉhā nimathā | vahilā pāve || Jñāne.5.32 || [Saṁ(n)nyāsastwiti] Yerā yogasthitī jayā sāṅḍe | to vāyāṅchi gā haVyāsī paḍe | parī prāpti kaṅhī na ghaḍe | saṅnyāsāchī || Jñāne.5.33 ||

Yogayukto vishuddhātmā vijitātmā jitendriyaḥ | sarvabhūtātmabhūtātmā kurvannapi na lipyate || Bha.Gī.5.7 ||

[Yogayukto vishuddhātmā] Jeṇe bhrāṅtīpāsūni hiratale | guruvākye mana dhutale | maga ātmaswarūpī ghātale | roūniyā || Jñāne.5.34 || Jaise samudrī lavaṇa na paḍe | tava vegaḷe alpa āvaḍe | maga hoya siṅdhūchiyevaḍhe | miḷe tevhā || Jñāne.5.35 || [Vijitātmā jitendriyaḥ] Taise saṅkalponi kāḍhile | jayāche manachi chaitanya ĵāhāle | [sarvabhūtātmabhūtātmā] teṇe ekadeshiye parī vyāpile | lokatraya || Jñāne.5.36 || [Kurvannapi na lipyate] Ātā kartā karma karāve | he khuṅṭale tayā swabhāve | āṇi karī jarhī āghave | tarhī akartā to || Jñāne.5.37 ||

Naiva kiñchitkaromīti yukto manyeta tattwavit | pashyañshrruṇvansprushañjighrannashnangachchhanswapañshwasan || Bha.Gī.5.8 || Pralapanvisrujangruhṇannunmiṣhannimiṣhannapi | indriyāṇīndriyārtheṣhu vartanta iti dhārayan || Bha.Gī.5.9 ||

[Naiva kiñchiditi] Je Pārthā tayā dehī | mī aisā āṭhavū nāhī | tarī kartrutwa kaiṅche kāī | ure sāṅge || Jñāne.5.38 || Aise tanutyāgeṁviṇa | amūrtāche guṇa | disatī saṁpūrṇa | yogayuktā || Jñāne.5.39 || Yerhavī āṇikāṅchiye parī | tohī eka sharīrī | asheṣhāhī vyāpārī | vartata dise || Jñāne.5.40 || [Pashyañshrruṇvansprushañjighran] Tohī netrī pāhe | shravaṇī aikata āhe | parī tethiṅchā sarvathā nohe | navala dekhe || Jñāne.5.41 || Sparshāsi tarī ĵāṇe | parimaḷa sevī ghrāṇe | [pralapan] avasarochita bolaṇe | tayāhi āthī || Jñāne.5.42 || [Ashnan gruhṇan iti padadwayavyākhyā] Āhārāte swīkārī | [visrujan] tyajāve te pariharī | [swapan] nidrechiyā avasarī | nidije sukhe || Jñāne.5.43 || [Gachchhan] Āpuleni ichchhāvashe | tohī gā chālata dise | pai sakaḷa karma aise | rāhāṭe kīra || Jñāne.5.44 || He sāṅgo kāi ekaika | [shwasan] dekhe shwāsochchhvāsādika | [unmiṣhannimiṣhannapi] āṇi nimiṣhonnimiṣha | ādikarūni || Jñāne.5.45 || Pārthā tayāche ṭhāyī | he āghaveṅchi āthi pāhī | parī to kartā navhe kāhī | pratītibaḷe || Jñāne.5.46 || Jai bhrāṅtiseje sutalā | tai swapnasukhe bhutalā | maga to jñānodayī cheilā | mhaṇoniyā || Jñāne.5.47 ||

Bramhaṇyādhāya karmāṇi saṅggaṁ(n) tyaktwā karoti yaḥ | lipyate na sa pāpena padmapatramivāmbhasā || Bha.Gī.5.10 ||

[Bramhaṇyādhāya karmāṇi] Ātā adhiṣhṭhānasaṅgatī | [indriyāṇīndriyārtheṣhwiti uttarārdha shlo. 9] asheṣhāhī iṅdriyavruttī | āpulāliyā arthī | vartata āhātī || Jñāne.5.48 || [Saṅggaṁ(n) tyaktwā karoti yaḥ] Dīpācheni prakāshe | gruhīṅche vyāpāra jaise | dehī karmajāta taise | yogayuktā || Jñāne.5.49 || To karme karī sakaḷe | [lipyate neti] parī karmabaṅdhā nākaḷe | jaise na siṅpe jaḷī jaḷe | padmapatra || Jñāne.5.50 ||

Kāyena manasā buddhyā kevalairindriyairapi | yoginaḥ(kh) karma kurvanti saṅggaṁ(n) tyaktwātmashuddhaye || Bha.Gī.5.11 ||

[Kāyena] Dekhe buddhīchī bhāṣha neṇije | manāchā aṅkura nudaije | aisā vyāpāra to bolije | shārīra gā || Jñāne.5.51 || Heṅcha marāṭhe pariyesī | tarī bāḷakāchī cheṣhṭā jaisī | yogiye karme karitī Yuktaḥ(kh) karmaphalaṁ(n) tyaktwā shāntimāpnoti naiṣhṭhikīm | ayuktaḥ(kh) kāmakāreṇa phale sakto nibadhyate || Bha.Gī.5.12 ||
