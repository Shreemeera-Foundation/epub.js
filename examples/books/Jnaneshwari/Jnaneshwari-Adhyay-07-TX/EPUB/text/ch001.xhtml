Adhyāya 7 vā

Shrībhagavānuvācha - Mayyāsaktamanāḥ(ph) Pārth yogaṁ yuñjanmadāshrayaḥ | asaṁshayaṁ samagraṁ māṁ yathā jñāsyasi tachchhruṇu || Bha.Gī.7.1 || Jñānaṁ(n) te’haṁ savijñānamidaṁ vakṣhyāmyasheṣhataḥ | yajjñātwā neha bhūyo’nyajjñātavyamavashiṣhyate || Bha.Gī.7.2 ||
